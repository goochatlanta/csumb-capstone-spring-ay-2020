What is GBBAT?

GDELT Browser-Based Access Tool

If you are at this site, then you likely already know about the dataset GDELT. But if you don't please click here.

We created GBBAT to help you access the massive GDELT dataset. The site is organized along a series of SQL queries you'll be performing on the three tables: events, eventmentions, and GKG.

This page offers you one query choice - to choose to look at the global newspace that covers events that involved an actor (one of two) from China.

Access Data (Query #1)

If you want to look at events & related news articles where a principle actor is of Chinese origin.
